Supported Layouts
====================

|          | History Chart | Stock/Crypto Logos | Full Ticker Name |
|:--------:|:-------------:|:------------------:|:----------------:|
| 32 × 16  |       ❌       |         ❌          |        ❌         |
| 64 × 32  |       ✅       |         ✅          |        ✅         |
| 128 × 64 |       ✅       |         ✅          |        ✅         |

Notes:
- Stock logos are supported for 64 × 32, but they might not look their best due to the lower resolution on the smaller 
board.
